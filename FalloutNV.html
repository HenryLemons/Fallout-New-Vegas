What is Fallout New Vegas about?

This will tell about the game genera of fallout new vegas, the games mechanics.

Fallout New Vegas is a apocalyptic survival rpg. It has stats from that help make diffrent builds for the game, and that makes each playthrough unique.

Skills

Fallout New Vegas has skills that will make you better and worse at things. The skills are Barter, Energy weapons, Explosives, Guns, Lockpick, Medicine, Melee Weapons, Repair, Science, Sneak, Speech, Survival, Unarmed. In this game skill checks are common for dialog and quests that use skills and SPECIAL. The player also tag 3 diffrent skills to add 15 more skill points in that skill. The stats in the begining is dependent on your SPECIAL.

SPECIAL

SPECIAL stands for strength, perception, endurance, charisma, intellegence, agility, and luck. Strength allows the coureir to carry more items and melee. Perception increases the chance you will hit your target in vats and modifies Explosives, Lockpick and Energy Weapons skills. Endurance determains how much hp you will have, how much poisen and radiation resistance, and it effects Unarmed and Survival. Charisma boosts Barter, Speech and Companion Nerve. Intellegence affects Skill Points you'll be able to distribute when you level up. Agility affects your Guns and Sneak skills, and the number of Action Points available for V.A.T.S. Luck increases critical strike chance and your odds of winning in a casino. SPECIAL gives 2 points or in its respective skills.

Weapons

Weapons in Fallout New Vegas have requirments to use them effectivly. it will have a strength requirment and the weapons respective skill to use. The image above shows the requirments. On the left it has a box with an energy sign with a 75 on it which means you need 75 in energy weapons to use it effectivly. On the right it shows STR 5 meaning you 5 strength to use the weapon effectivly. Below the energy requirment is the damage and dps, and it shows how much damage you do in one shot and one second. Below the dps is the condition of the weapon, and if the condition of the weapon is less than the pinched section it will do less damage and jam more often. On the right of the condition will show what amunition it will take. With amunition there are many types of amunition that you can use and make. The amunition will have diffrent effects like working better with light armor enemies but worse on heavy armor enemies. And when you fire the weapon and it takes amunition you have a chance to get either a shell casing or empty cell depending if its a gun or energy weapon.
