Brief Starting knowledge for Fallout New Vegas.

Fallout New Vegas is a survival post-apocalyptic rpg with multiple endings. It was made Obsidian Studios, and was inspired by the original topdown turn-based rpg's fallout 1 and fallout 2. With those games as an insparation and Fallout 3 being out, Obsidian studio took both the Fallout 3 base gameplay and the randomness of the original games. What is Fallout New Vegas about?

Fallout New Vegas is a apocalyptic survival rpg. It has stats from that help make diffrent builds for the game, and that makes each playthrough unique.

Skills

Fallout New Vegas has skills that will make you better and worse at things. The skills are Barter, Energy weapons, Explosives, Guns, Lockpick, Medicine, Melee Weapons, Repair, Science, Sneak, Speech, Survival, Unarmed. In this game skill checks are common for dialog and quests that use skills and SPECIAL. The player also tag 3 diffrent skills to add 15 more skill points in that skill. The stats in the begining is dependent on your SPECIAL.

SPECIAL

SPECIAL stands for strength, perception, endurance, charisma, intellegence, agility, and luck. Strength allows the coureir to carry more items and melee. Perception increases the chance you will hit your target in vats and modifies Explosives, Lockpick and Energy Weapons skills. Endurance determains how much hp you will have, how much poisen and radiation resistance, and it effects Unarmed and Survival. Charisma boosts Barter, Speech and Companion Nerve. Intellegence affects Skill Points you'll be able to distribute when you level up. Agility affects your Guns and Sneak skills, and the number of Action Points available for V.A.T.S. Luck increases critical strike chance and your odds of winning in a casino. SPECIAL gives 2 points or in its respective skills.

Armor

There are diffrent types of armor in Fallout New Vegas and each armor type has its perks and detrements. There is Light, Medium, Heavy, Power Armor, and clothing.

Light Armor

This armor has low weight and low dt.Wearing any light armor (including clothing) incurs no additional penalties.

Medium Armor

This armor has medium weight with medium dt. Wearing medium armor causes the wearer to move 7.5% slower and have their effective sneak reduced by 10.

Heavy Armor

This armor has high weight but great dt. Wearing heavy armor causes the wearer to move 15% slower and have their effective sneak reduced by 20.

Power Armor

Power armor is a heavy armor that needs training to use and there are two ways to getting it is through the brotherhood of steel, and a companion quest. The only power armor that doesn't need training is the NCR salvaged power armor.

Clothing

Most wearable items that are classed as clothing (such as hats and eyewear) cannot be repaired by merchants that offer repair services. This requires you to either use compatible clothing in the item's class or select the Jury Rigging perk to repair clothing items.

Damage resistance

Damage resistance is calculated before DT. Things that give you Damage Resistance is Med-X, Battle Brew, and Slasher. There are only 3 armors that give Damage resistance and its the Vault 11 jumpsuit that gives 1, the rebreather that gives 3, and Scientist outfit that gives 3.

Damage Threshold

DT can function as an outright subtraction: if a shot is fired with a damage value of 40 against a DT of 10, then 10 damage will be ignored. However, the sequence of calculations differs compared to previous games. DR is applied first. The value after this adjustment depends on any DR a player character has, which is capped to a DR of 85 (85%); however, DR above 80 does nothing, so the theoretical cap is 80.

Ammo types

In Fallout New Vegas has diffrent types of ammo for diffrent types of situations. The diffrent types of guns also have options for ammo. For coventinal guns was the regular ammo you get off of enemys while surplus can only be bought and decreases the guns condition a lot faster. There are also hollow point for enemys that have low dt and does more damage but for more armored enemies it does little, but armor piercing is used for those enemies. For explosive guns there are a multitude of explosives you can choose from that are grenade types. For energy weapons you have increased charges and damage for each shot, but it takes more weapon condition away. With this many options for guns and more options with ammo, you have a lot of choices you can make each playthrough.

Types of damage

There are many types of damage in fallout new vegas. There is regular damage from bullets and melee attacks, fire damage from flamers that deal damage over time, fall damage is from falling long distances, and effect damage that takes an interger amount of health over time and sometimes can be cured. Radiation is another form of damage of its own with its own health bar of 1,000. Every 200 rads will cause ill effects that reduce your special stats, and at 200 rads it takes 1 END away. At 400 -2 END, -1 AGL. At 600 rads -3 END, -2 AGL, -1 STR. At 800 rads -3 END, -2 AGL, -2 STR. And at 1000 rads you will die. Pulse is used for robots and does more damage to them and their limbs.

Big Factions

There are two big factions in Fallout New Vegas. The NCR and the Caesar's Legion. Warning: I am biased so take this with a grain of salt from the white legs home.

The NCR

The NCR is a republic and is an abbreviation of New California Republic. The NCR stands for pre-war morals and government, so taxes, laws, and corruption is a part of life in the NCR regions. The NCR is fighting for hover damn so they can send power to the east coast of the US. And when the game takes place the NCR has control of hover damn but the legion is preparing an attack to take the damn. As a republic they have a president named Aaron Kimball.

Caesar's Legion

The legion stands for the even older values of rome with some twists. They are slavers, raiders, but they don't allow chems like buffout and pshyco. They asimilate tribes and make them think that they were barbarians and the legion civilised them. They are ruled by Caeser, a man with a brain tumor and many npc's say that the legion will fall when Caesar dies.

Small Factions mentioned

Powder Gangers

Conficts that escaped the NCR's rehabilitation camp. They are violent with dynamite.

GoodSprings

Towns folk of GoodSprings that are willing to help anyone. They are the tutorial town of this game.

Great Khans

A tribe of drug makers and raiders. They want to seek their previous glory and get revenge on the NCR for their wrongful genocide agaist their people.

The Kings

A group of elvis impersonators that act like the law enforcement of freeside. They are ruled by The King who is resonable and you can get a favor from.

The Followers of the Apocalypse

A group of educated people who want nothing else but to help others rebuild societys good parts and offer help with little to no cost to others. They are true and just people.

Why are there factions to begin with.

Unlike in fallout 3, Fallout New Vegas has multiple endings you can get with each decision you make impacting the world you inhabit. Having factions, big and small, makes even killing people affect the game by gaining faction approval or disapproval. Thus by adding factions and making each faction either like you or hate you makes the game more imersive and the story more interactable than fallout 3. There are more faction than the ones i've listed but you can find them when you are playing the game.
